## [Released]

## [0.1.0] - 2021-05-02

### Added

- All the default methods:
  - `get(key)`
  - `set(key, value)`
  - `delete(key)`
  - `list(prefix)`
- The extra methods from the official Node.js client:
  - `empty()`
  - `get_all()`
  - `set_all(hash)`
  - `delete_multiple(keys)`
